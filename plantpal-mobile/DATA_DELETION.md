# PlantPal — Data Deletion Policy

Users can request deletion of their PlantPal account and associated data.

## What gets deleted

When an account is deleted, we remove or anonymize:

- Supabase Auth user record
- Plants, care logs, and photos linked to that user
- Profile and preference data
- Feedback submissions tied to the account (optional retention of anonymized feedback)

Server-side scan logs and aggregated analytics may be retained in anonymized form.

## How to request deletion

### In app (when available)
Settings → Account → Delete my account

### By email
Send a request to **support@getplantpal.com** from the email address on your PlantPal account.

Include:
- Subject: `Delete my PlantPal account`
- Your registered email address
- Confirmation that you want all plant data removed

We will confirm deletion within **30 days** (typically within 7 business days).

## What we cannot delete from third parties

If you used AI scan features, images may have been processed by OpenAI or Pl@ntNet under their retention policies. We do not control third-party retention after processing completes.

## Partial deletion

You may delete individual plants from the Garden tab without deleting your entire account.

## Google Play & App Store

This policy satisfies store requirements for user data deletion. Link this document or the equivalent section on https://getplantpal.com/privacy in your store listings.
