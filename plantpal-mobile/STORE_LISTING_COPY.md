## Full description

PlantPal is your smart plant care coach. Track every plant you own, identify plants with your camera, diagnose problems with photos, get ZIP-code-based care advice, complete daily plant tasks, and learn gardening through PlantPal Academy.

**Track your garden**
Add plants by scanning, searching, or typing. See health, size, location, and goals at a glance.

**Scan & identify**
Use your phone camera with Live AI and Pl@ntNet to identify plants and review confidence scores.

**Daily care**
See what needs watering or attention today — personalized to your plants.

**PlantPal Academy**
Short lessons on watering, pests, fruit trees, and more.

**Local intelligence**
Your ZIP code powers weather-aware care tips.

PlantPal can make mistakes. Use AI guidance as a starting point, not certainty.
